# Picnic Style Guide

Reference for AI agents writing copy or generating images for Picnic.

---

## Part 1 — Copywriting

### Brand

- **Name**: Picnic
- **Tagline**: Suas finanças no modo Picnic.
- **One-liner**: A conta digital internacional mais inteligente do Brasil.
- **Archetype**: Explorador Insurgente — a sophisticated challenger that questions traditional financial systems without being aggressively rebellious.
- **Personality**: Modern, playful, expressive, young & innovative.

### Brand Pillars

| Pillar | Description | Emotional hook |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Transparência Radical | No hidden fees. No fine print. What you see is what you pay. | Sem taxa escondida. Sem pegadinha. |
| Inteligência Tecnológica | Blockchain tech abstracted into a simple fintech experience. | Tecnologia que trabalha pra você, não contra. |
| Liberdade Consciente | Financial freedom for travelers — control, autonomy, no dependency on legacy systems. | Seu dinheiro, seu de verdade. |

### Audience

- **Primary**: Viajante Frequente Brasileiro (30–50, class AB, 2+ international trips/year). Digitally fluent, compares options, hates hidden fees.
- **Secondary**: USD Earners & Capital Protectors (devs/entrepreneurs earning in USD).

### Voice

**Casual but substantive.** Like a smart friend sharing a financial hack — not a bank talking down to you, not a crypto bro hyping you up.

| Trait | Do | Don't |
|-------|------|--------|
| Direto | "IOF? Zero. Taxa? Zero. Pegadinha? Zero." | "Nós nos orgulhamos de oferecer uma experiência financeira completamente isenta de taxas ocultas." |
| Confiante sem ser arrogante | "5–10% mais barato que Wise. Faz a conta." | "Somos MUITO melhores que a concorrência!!!" |
| Leve mas com substância | "Viajar sem susto no extrato." | "Viaje pelo mundo com a gente! 🌍✨🎉" |
| Insider, não vendedor | "Descobri um jeito mais inteligente de viajar." | "Compre agora e aproveite!" |
| Coloquial brasileiro natural | "Abre o olho pro custo total, sério." | "Atente-se ao custo total de suas transações internacionais." |

### Register

- **Formality**: informal to neutral — never formal/corporate
- **Pronoun**: "você" (never "tu", never "o senhor")
- **Contractions**: use freely — "tá", "pra", "pro", "tava", "num"
- **Slang**: moderate — natural Brazilian internet-speak is fine, heavy gíria is not

### Copy Principles

Three frameworks in priority order:

1. **Harry Dry's 3 Rules**: Can I visualize it? Can I falsify it? Can only WE say this?
2. **Light Copy (Fernando Ladeira)**: Premises over promises. Suggest, don't command. Use literary elements (irony, imagery, contrast).
3. **7 Técnicas**: Strong opening. Show don't tell. Be underground. Repetition with purpose.

### Quality Checklist

- [ ] Can someone understand this in 2 seconds? (Teste dos 2 Mississippis)
- [ ] Am I showing a concrete image or stating an abstract adjective?
- [ ] Does this copy have contrast/conflict built in?
- [ ] Could a competitor say the exact same thing? If yes, rewrite.
- [ ] Did I cut every unnecessary word? (Kaplan's Law)
- [ ] Is this premises-based or promises-based?
- [ ] Would someone want to share this, or does it feel like an ad?

### Content Patterns

**Contrast formula** — the core persuasion structure:
> [What competitors/banks do wrong] → [What Picnic does differently]
>
> "Tem cartão dizendo zero IOF… mas cobrando escondido."
> "Os outros prometem. O Picnic mostra a conta."

**Zero List** — brand signature, use every 2–3 pieces:
> IOF: zero. Taxa Picnic: zero. Anuidade: zero. Pegadinha: zero.

**Mental images** — at least one per piece:
- Passar o cartão torcendo pra não vir caro.
- Descobrir taxa escondida só depois, no extrato.
- Abrir o app e ver exatamente quanto tá pagando.
- Pagar tudo em outro país sem travar: café, metrô, mercado.
- Viajar leve, sem paranoia de cartão.

**Brand signatures** — use 1–2 per piece:
- Se aceita Visa, aceita Picnic.
- Picnic: zero taxa de verdade.
- Abre o olho pro custo total.
- Seu dinheiro, seu de verdade.
- O cartão que funciona, ponto.
- Viajar sem susto no extrato.

### Product Claims (safe to use)

- **Zero IOF** — always safe, this is structural
- **5–10% cheaper than Wise** — use with evidence/calculator
- **Up to 5% cashback** — always say "até", never guarantee max
- **Visa® accepted in 180 countries** — include ® on first mention
- **Pix deposit in minutes** — avoid exact minute promises
- **USD yield** — avoid "investimento" or specific % guarantees in app copy

### Forbidden

**Words** (in user-facing copy): blockchain, USDC, stablecoin, crypto/criptomoeda, dólar comercial, garantido, investimento (in card/travel context), custo real de conversão, sem risco, o melhor/o mais barato.

**Tones**: Conspiratório, agressivo contra concorrência, bom demais pra ser verdade, corporativo/banco tradicional, crypto bro.

**Anti-patterns**: Walls of text, generic fintech copy, promises without evidence, exclamation mark overload (!!!), emoji spam, starting copy with the brand name.

### Writing Rules

- **Sentences**: max 15 words, prefer 8–12
- **Paragraphs**: max 2 lines in marketing
- **Punctuation**: periods for rhythm, fragments OK, questions create engagement
- **Numbers**: digits not words — "5 minutos" not "cinco minutos"
- **Specificity**: "R$1.439 em uma viagem de US$5.000" > "economize bastante"
- **Reading level**: write for a smart 16-year-old

### App UI Copy

- Max 2 lines per screen element
- Action-oriented: tell users what happens, not what to think
- Error messages: helpful and human, never scary
- Success states: rewarding but not over-the-top
- **Buttons**: first person / infinitive — "Consultar", "Ativar" (never imperative "Consulte")
- **Body text**: third person / neutral — describes what the user sees

### Competitive Positioning

Compare with evidence, not insults. Be the smart alternative, not the angry disruptor.

- **vs Wise**: 5–10% cheaper on total cost. Show the math.
- **vs Nomad**: Similar audience, structural cost advantage via blockchain rails.
- **vs Nubank**: Charges IOF + spread. Picnic eliminates both.
- **vs banks**: IOF (up to 5.38%) + spread + annual fees → Picnic: zero across the board.
- **Framing**: never "the cheapest" (commodity trap) → "the smartest way to spend internationally"

## Part 2 — Image Generation (Lemon Pie Style)

### Overview

**Lemon Pie Style (LPS)** is Picnic's default illustration style. Bold yet minimal, retro charm with modern clarity.

### Workflow

1. Simple prompt (e.g., "piggy bank with coins")
2. Enrich with LPS style definitions below
3. Send to Freepik Mystic API (model: `fluid`, resolution: `1k`)
4. Return URL or add to project

### Style Rules

- **Colors** (hex): #ffffff, #f7f3ed, #eeeadf, #cdf45f, #88be37, #53ac7a, #04916f, #007054, #105644, #202108. Accents: #F7BAC4, #E98771. Backgrounds: flat solid or plain white.
- **Linework**: Selective black lines for contrast/separation only. No cartoon outlines. Variable thickness.
- **Texture**: Subtle risograph grain and brushstroke, applied sparingly. No heavy noise/grunge.
- **Characters**: Exaggerated proportions (large hands, wide shoulders, thick limbs, simplified heads). Minimal stylized faces. Expressive body language. Color-blocked clothing. Diverse and inclusive.
- **Composition**: Medium close-up or full body. Center stage, minimal background. Energetic diagonals. Extra breathing room for UI. Don't crop upper limbs.
- **Poses**: Slight gesture or action. Never stiff or perfectly symmetrical.
- **Influences**: Retro print textures, children's book illustrations, contemporary editorial, Memphis-style graphics, flat design.
- **Vibe**: Friendly, creative, collaborative. Joy, teamwork, movement, expression.

### Prompt Template

```
[subject description], flat illustration style, bold minimal design, retro charm with modern clarity, flat color blocks in chartreuse green (#cdf45f) and deep greens (#04916f, #007054), selective black lines for contrast only, subtle risograph grain texture, stylized exaggerated proportions, expressive pose, [solid color] background, extra breathing room, contemporary editorial illustration, Memphis-style influence, clean and friendly
```

### Negative Prompt Template

```
realistic photo, 3D render, text, watermark, blurry, dark moody lighting, heavy grunge texture, cartoon outlines around shapes, stiff symmetrical pose, complex detailed background, cropped upper limbs
```
